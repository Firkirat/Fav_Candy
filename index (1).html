My Favorite Candy: Pulse Candy

Interesting Facts

Pulse, officially known as Pass Pass Pulse, is a hard candy manufactured by Noida-based DS Group and marketed under its umbrella brand 'Pass Pass'. Originally launched in a most sold 'Kachcha Aam' flavour, the hard-boiled candy is filled with tangy salt and spices in its central core.
